Apple iBook G4
The Apple iBook G4 was a line of laptop computers made by Apple Inc. between October 2003 and May 2006. Known for its durable design and powerful performance, the iBook G4 became a favorite among students and professionals.
Specifications
  • Processor: PowerPC G4
  • Display: 12.1" or 14.1" TFT
  • Memory: 256MB, expandable to 1.25GB
  • Storage: 30GB, 40GB, 60GB or 80GB HDD
  • Graphics: ATI Mobility Radeon 9200
  • Operating System: macOS
